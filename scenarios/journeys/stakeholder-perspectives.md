## 1. Investors / Limited Partners (LPs)

### Who They Are
- Pension funds
- Sovereign wealth funds
- Insurance companies
- Endowments
- Family offices
- High net worth individuals

### What They Care About

| Priority | Concern | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Returns (absolute and relative to benchmark) | Quarterly |
| 2 | Risk (portfolio, concentration, market) | Quarterly |
| 3 | Liquidity (distributions, capital calls) | Quarterly |
| 4 | ESG/Sustainability (ratings, compliance) | Annual |
| 5 | Governance (alignment, transparency) | Ongoing |

### Information Needs

**Quarterly:**
- NAV and performance attribution
- Portfolio composition and changes
- Market outlook and strategy
- Risk metrics (leverage, concentration, currency)

**Annual:**
- Detailed financial statements
- ESG report and ratings
- Strategy review
- Manager assessment

**Ad-hoc:**
- Specific exposures (sector, tenant, geography)
- Scenario analysis
- Peer comparisons
- Due diligence for re-ups

### Pain Points

1. **Information asymmetry:** Manager knows more than LP
2. **Reporting delays:** Data is 30-60 days old
3. **Comparability:** Every manager reports differently
4. **ESG verification:** Greenwashing concerns
5. **Access to underlying data:** Aggregated only

### AI Value Opportunities

| Opportunity | Value | Sensitivity |
|-------------|-------|-------------|
| Custom report generation | Faster, tailored responses | Low |
| Real-time exposure analysis | Better risk management | Medium |
| Peer benchmarking | Better decision making | High (data sharing) |
| ESG verification | Trust in claims | Medium |
| Natural language queries | Self-service | Low |

### AI Trust Requirements
- Accuracy is paramount (fiduciary context)
- Audit trail for all data
- Clear methodology disclosure
- Independent verification possible

## AI System Design Implications

### Multi-Tenancy Requirements
- Strict data isolation between funds/clients
- Role-based access control within organizations
- Audit trail for all access
- Clear data ownership

### Stakeholder-Specific Interfaces
- Different views for different users
- Appropriate detail levels
- Role-appropriate actions
- Clear permission boundaries

### Trust and Verification
- Source citations for all data
- Calculation methodology transparency
- Human approval for material outputs
- Error rate visibility

### Professional Liability Clarity
- AI as assistant, not decision maker
- Clear disclaimers
- Professional review requirements
- Audit trail for advice-related outputs
