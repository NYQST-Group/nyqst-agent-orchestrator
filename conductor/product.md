# Initial Concept
Intelli is an agent-first document intelligence platform for commercial real estate (CRE) workflows. It focuses on single-source-of-truth provenance, ephemeral compute with persistent outputs, and policy-driven governance.

## Vision
To provide a secure, tenant-isolated, enterprise-ready platform that leverages agents and LLMs for document ingestion, analysis, and report generation (starting with a Lease CD MVP) while maintaining strict provenance and compliance.

## Target Users
- Commercial Real Estate (CRE) Analysts
- Deal Teams and Lenders
- Enterprise Administrators

## Core Modules
- **Substrate**: Immutable artifacts and manifests.
- **Orchestration**: Agent execution via LangGraph.
- **Knowledge**: RAG and doc parsing via Docling.
- **Export**: Deterministic Excel/PDF outputs.
